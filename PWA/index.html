Goals of Gender Equality

Ensuring that all genders have equal legal rights and protections, including in areas such as employment, education, healthcare, and political participation.

Ensuring equitable access to healthcare services, including reproductive health services, maternal care, and mental health support, regardless of gender.

Promoting education and awareness about gender equality issues to foster a more inclusive and respectful society.
